# Code of Conduct

This is a small open-source project. The bar is simple: be a decent person.

---

## The short version

Treat everyone with respect. This community is open to people of all backgrounds, skill levels, and experience. Newcomers are especially welcome — everyone started somewhere.

---

## What we expect

- Talk to people the way you'd want to be talked to
- Disagree on ideas, not on people
- Accept that feedback on your code isn't feedback on you
- Help people who are learning — don't make them feel stupid for asking

## What we won't tolerate

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks — public or private
- Sexualised language or imagery
- Sharing someone's private information without permission
- Any behaviour that would get you thrown out of a professional setting

---

## Reporting

If something happens that shouldn't have, email **hello@safeenv.dev**. Reports are handled privately and taken seriously.

---

## Consequences

Depending on severity:

1. **Warning** — a private explanation of what went wrong and why
2. **Temporary ban** — time-limited removal from all community spaces
3. **Permanent ban** — for serious or repeated violations
